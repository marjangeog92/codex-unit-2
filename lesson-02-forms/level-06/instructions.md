## Level 06 — Radios & Checkboxes

Objective: Implement radio groups and checkboxes, ensuring radios that belong to the same group share a `name`.

Tasks:

- Add two or more radio inputs that share the same `name` attribute.
- Add at least one checkbox input for multi-selection.

Hints:

- Radios that share a `name` behave as a single-choice group.
