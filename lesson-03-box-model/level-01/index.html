Your notes

The boxc model is described from the inside out. It contains 4 layers. The first layer is the content layer. Text and images appear here. The 2nd layer is padding which is the space inside the border between the content and the border. The 3rd layer is the border which is a laine around the padding and content. The 4th and final layer is the margin. This is outer space separating this entire element from others.
